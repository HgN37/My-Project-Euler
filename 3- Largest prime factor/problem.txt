__Problem: Largest prime factor
The prime factors of 13195 are 5, 7, 13 and 29.
What is the largest prime factor of the number 600851475143 ?

__Answer: 6857

__Solution: divide the given number by an increasing number start from 2. Each
time the division's remainder is not 0, skip and increase the divisor by 1. When
the divident equal 1, stop, the value of the divisor is the largest prime factor 
now.

__By the way: 600851475143 = 71 x 839 x 1471 x 6857
